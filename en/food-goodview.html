Good View Bar & Restaurant

The location is named the most beautiful. Charoenrat on the banks of the Ping River between Nawarat Bridge. On January 19, 1996, Nakorn Ping Bridge was the first place to be named in the name of THE GOOD VIEW. The restaurant serves international food such as Thai, Chinese, Western and Japanese with many kinds of drinks. The interior is decorated with modern Thai style to make it easy for guests to come. Enjoyed the atmosphere fully. There are more than 120 customer service points available to serve more than 800 customers on a regular basis. Of course, every visitor to this place will appreciate the Ping River at night. A live band will entertain you every night. And also have a concert. From the artist a lot of people continue. THE GOOD VIEW has become a symbol of Chiang Mai. It is well known among both Thai and foreign tourists. From mouth to mouth, THE GOOD VIEW has been popular until now.
